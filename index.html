About GoldenBite

GoldenBite was founded with a simple mission: to serve delicious fast food made with quality ingredients. What started as a small food truck in 2010 has grown into multiple locations across the country.

We take pride in our handcrafted burgers, artisanal pizzas, and signature sides that are made fresh daily. Our commitment to quality and taste has made us a favorite among food lovers.
